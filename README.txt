Collaborated with New Britain Cares and ParaDYM Academy through the CCSU Software Engineering Studio to deploy the first launch of the New Britain Cares Mobile Passport for Success. 

Passport for Success serves as a roadmap to self-sufficiency, defined as lower reliance on federal and state subsidies for low income residents in the city of New Britain. 

The product conducts several assessments and aggregate data to illustrate any progress being made by participants. The aggregated data will then be used to assess the individual successes of participants and the overall success of the program. 
Technologies used: React.js, Firebase & Expo

Available on App Store (iOS) & Google Play (Android) June 2022
